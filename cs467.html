Motivation

We want to create a way to play music via touch based interaction. It should be so easy anyone can use it. And, most importantly, it has to be fun.

Audience

Anyone who enjoys playing with music.

Process

Tactaphone works on most touch based mobile devices. You can touch on the canvas that we provide to you to generate some music.
