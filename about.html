You can find Teng Siong Ong aka siong1987 on Twitter or send him an email.

Zach works at GitHub. When he's not doing that, he's blogging, hacking on open source, and filming screencasts that are anything but "traditional" screencasts. He attracted national media fame and glory when he built Facelette, a ridiculous three-hour experiment, and lost all of it again after admitting he sometimes stays home on Friday nights and replies to support emails.

Zach has also been working on Good-Tutorials since 2002. It's amassed tens of thousands of technology tutorials over the last decade. Good-Tutorials has been featured on the BBC, SkyNews, TechTV (now G4), PC World, and other fine journalistic endeavors.

If you have any questions or feedback, check out the issues on holman/feedback on GitHub. It's sort of like an "Ask Me Anything" setup, except it uses a nerdy social network and crams it into a bug tracker.
